// CAPABILITIES

What We Detect

🤖
AI-Generated Images

Detects images made by Midjourney, DALL-E, Stable Diffusion, and other generative models using GAN fingerprint analysis, unnatural noise patterns, and frequency domain checks.

→ GAN DETECTION
🎭
Deepfake Videos

Frame-by-frame analysis for face-swap artifacts, blending inconsistencies, unnatural blinking, lip-sync errors, and temporal flickering that expose synthetic faces.

→ FACE ANALYSIS
📅
Old Video Reuse

Reverse image/video search against global news archives. Flags when footage is presented out of context — a key propaganda tactic used in war disinformation campaigns.

→ REVERSE SEARCH
✂️
Editing & Manipulation

Error Level Analysis (ELA) and metadata forensics identify spliced images, color grading, cropped or added objects, and inconsistent EXIF timestamps.

→ ELA FORENSICS
🛡️
Identity Protection

Every submission creates an encrypted uploader record with IP hash, timestamp, and SHA-256 file signature. Provides legal-grade evidence to clear innocent people falsely accused in leaks.

→ BLOCKCHAIN ANCHOR
🗺️
Geolocation Verification

Cross-references claimed location with satellite imagery, weather data, shadow angle, and architectural recognition to verify if a video was actually recorded where claimed.

→ GEO-VERIFICATION

Who This Protects

TruthLens is built for real victims of media manipulation — not just researchers.

😰
MMS & Leak Victims

People falsely accused of uploading intimate media or whose images are used without consent. TruthLens creates a verifiable legal record.

  • Timestamped upload record proves you did NOT upload content
  • SHA-256 file hash creates immutable evidence
  • Legal-grade report for law enforcement or lawyers
  • Identity encrypted — only released by court order
📰
Journalists & Fact-Checkers

Verify breaking news media before publishing. Flag footage being recycled as new content for propaganda or clickbait.

  • Reverse-search against 500M+ archived news images
  • Pinpoint original source and publication date
  • Export shareable verdict for published corrections
  • API access for bulk verification workflows
⚔️
War & Conflict Contexts

Old footage recycled as current events is one of the most dangerous forms of disinformation. We identify when and where videos actually originate.

  • Archive cross-reference with conflict journalism databases
  • Geolocation verification against satellite data
  • Weather and shadow-angle temporal analysis
  • Flag videos with mismatched claimed location/date

General Public & Activists

Anyone exposed to viral content that seems suspicious. Stop misinformation from spreading before it causes real-world harm.

  • One-click verification for any viral image or clip
  • Shareable "VERIFIED FAKE" / "VERIFIED REAL" badges

  • Free to use — no account required for basic checks
